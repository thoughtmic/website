Privacy is not a feature. It's the whole product.

Your voice and your vault never leave your Mac. Everything else is opt-in and independently verifiable.

  • Voice recordings and audio
  • Vault contents, files, tags, links
  • Dictation history
  • Whisper model and settings
  • Generated titles, tags, backlinks
  • App names you dictate into

Opt-in only

  • Rephrasing text (Pro) — transcript only, never audio or vault. ZDR enabled.
